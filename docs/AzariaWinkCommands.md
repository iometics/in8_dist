# Azaria Wink Command System: Explorer's Guide

## Introduction to Azaria

Welcome, traveler, to the mysterious world of Azaria - a realm of lost cultures, forgotten technologies, and ancient wisdom waiting to be rediscovered. As you embark on your journey through this enigmatic landscape, you'll need to master the arcane art of the Wink Command System - secret instructions that allow you to navigate, investigate, and interact with the world around you.

All wink commands begin with a semicolon (`;`) - the ancient symbol that signifies you're communicating with the deeper systems of Azaria rather than simply conversing with its inhabitants.

## Navigation & Exploration

### Basic Movement
- `;[location]` - Travel to a specific location (e.g., `;village`, `;city`)
- `;where` - Reveals your current location in the world
- `;pos` - Determines your exact coordinates within the current view
- `;dpos` - Shows the distance traveled since your last position check
- `;bearing` - Provides your directional bearing (0-359 degrees)
- `;facing` - Reveals your compass heading

### Advanced Exploration
- `;mark [name]` - Creates a waypoint at your current location with a custom name
- `;find [player]` - Locates another explorer in Azaria
- `;look [player]` - Observes another explorer from afar
- `;follow [player]` - Allows you to trail behind another explorer in their travels

## Social Interactions

### Communication
- `;font [style]` - Changes your communication style
  - Available styles: `tnr`, `parent`, `village`, `city`, `big`, `small`

### Emotes
Express yourself with these emotional gestures:
- `;Happy` - Express joy
- `;Sad` - Show sorrow
- `;Mad` - Display anger
- `;Surprised` - Demonstrate astonishment
- `;Kiss` - Offer affection
- `;Wave` - Greet others
- `;Hurt` - Indicate pain
- `;Stress` - Show anxiety

### Multiplayer
- `;join [session]` - Connect to another expedition group
- `;share` - Exchange important findings with other explorers

## Object Interaction

### Items & Inventory
- `;get [item]` - Attempt to acquire an object (restricted to certain explorers)
- `;got` - Check what you're currently holding

### Environment Interaction
- `;finger [object/location]` - Examine something closely
- `;rig` - Interact with mechanisms or devices
- `;unload [view]` - Release a particular view from your perception

## Advanced Tools for Scholars

### View Management
- `;add view [name]` - Create a new perspective on the world
- `;status` - Check the condition of your current viewpoints

### Debug & Analysis
- `;mon [all|mach|none]` - Toggle observation of machinery
- `;trace [machine|all]` - Follow the workings of Azarian mechanisms
- `;log [module] [level]` - Adjust the detail level of your expedition journal
  - Modules: `AUDIO`, `TELETYPE`, `NETWORK`, `VIEWS`
  - Levels: `NONE`, `CRITICAL`, `ERROR`, `WARNING`, `INFO`, `ALL`

### Experimental Commands
*These commands access deeper systems of Azaria and may have unpredictable results*

- `;reset [machine]` - Return a mechanism to its original state
- `;signal [machine] [signal]` - Send coded instructions to Azarian machinery
- `;sql` - Record the structure of your current view
- `;ws [code]` - Establish a mysterious connection with Azarian communication systems

### Mystic Utilities
- `;run [filename]` - Execute a sequence of recorded actions
- `;replay` - Reset and replay the recorded transactions
- `;wealth` - A peculiar command said to influence one's fortunes

## Help System
- `;help` or `;?` - Displays basic help information
- `;help x` - Reveals additional arcane commands
