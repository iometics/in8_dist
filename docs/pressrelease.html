What ioMetics is looking at for an upcoming release of in8

DATE: February 1, 2026
SUBJECT: Advancing Sovereign Connectivity: Cryptographic Roadmap

Olympia, WA — ioMetics is pleased to announce the core technical pillars currently under development for the next major release of in8, our comprehensive C++ peer-to-peer (P2P) application development system.

Strategic Development Focus: "The Invisible Network"

The next iteration of in8 is designed to solve the two most critical challenges in modern decentralized systems: Unobservable Discovery and Contextual Trust.

1. Zero-Knowledge Discovery (ZKD) Implementation of Lattice-based PAKE to allow peers to find and authenticate without exposing metadata to public DHTs. This ensures a "Shadow Network" visibility for private family groups.
2. Threshold Cryptography & Group Sovereignty Utilizing BBS+ Group Signatures to enable "Inside Trust, Outside Anonymity." Facilitates high-trust discussions where the group remains a black box to the public.
3. Hybrid Post-Quantum Resilience A dual-layer approach combining Curve25519 with ML-KEM (Kyber) to secure today’s data against tomorrow’s quantum threats.

Distributed State via CRDTs

To power seamless P2P gaming and collaborative tools, the new release features Conflict-free Replicated Data Types with per-field encryption. This ensures synchronized state across intermittent links without a central authority.

— ioMetics Engineering
